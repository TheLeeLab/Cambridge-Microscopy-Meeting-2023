The Cambridge Microscopy Meeting 2023 (CMM2023) is a meeting organised for and by microscopy developers in Cambridge. It will be held on 11th–12th December 2023 at the Yusuf Hamied Department of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge. Thanks to our amazing sponsors, the meeting will be free to attend, but registration is required.

Important dates

Abstract submission opens Mon, 7 Aug 2023
Abstract submission closes Fri, 20 Oct 2023 (23:59 BST)
Programme announced Fri, 27 Oct 2023
Meeting dates 11-12 December 2023
