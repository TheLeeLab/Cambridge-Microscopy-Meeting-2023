The Cambridge Microscopy Meeting 2023 (CMM2023) is a meeting organised for and by microscopy developers in Cambridge. It will be held on 11-12 December 2023 at the Yusuf Hamied Department of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge. The meeting is free to attend, but registration is required.

Important dates

Abstract submission opens Mon, 7 Aug 2023
Abstract submission closes Fri, 13 Oct 2023 (23:59 BST)
Programme announced Fri, 20 Oct 2023
Meeting dates 11-12 December 2023
